Sonic the Hedgehog by SEGA

Sonic the Hedgehog is a Japanese video game series and media franchise created and owned by Sega. The franchise follows Sonic, an anthropomorphic blue hedgehog who battles the evil Doctor Eggman, a mad scientist. He also runs at supersonic speeds and can curl into a ball, primarily to attack various enemies. In most games, Sonic must race through levels, collecting power-up rings and avoiding obstacles and enemies.
